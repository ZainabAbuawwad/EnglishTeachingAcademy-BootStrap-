Exam Mastery: IELTS & TOEFL

Home / Courses / Exam Mastery

IELTS / TOEFL Level: C1 target Popular

Six weeks of focused, examiner-informed preparation for IELTS and TOEFL. You'll sit weekly timed mock tests, get section-by-section feedback on Writing and Speaking, and leave with a study plan built around your target score.

What you'll walk away with
A realistic target-band strategy for Reading, Writing, Listening & Speaking
4 full-length timed mock exams with examiner-style scoring
Personalized written feedback on every Task 1 & Task 2 essay
Recorded speaking sessions with band-by-band breakdown

Baseline mock test, skimming/scanning strategy, note-taking for Listening Section 3–4, and common trap-question patterns.

Data description structures, essay planning under time pressure, and cohesive-device drills with 1:1 written feedback.

Part 1–3 practice, filler-word reduction, and recorded sessions reviewed with an examiner-trained tutor.

A complete timed exam under real conditions, scored the same day.

Targeted drills on your lowest-scoring section, plus a final study plan to take into exam day.
