Our story

We got tired of English classes that taught grammar, not conversation

FluentUp started with a simple frustration: talented students who could pass every grammar test still froze the moment they had to actually speak. So we built an academy around speaking first — grammar becomes the thing that supports the conversation, not the goal of it.

Practical over perfect

We'd rather you speak with a few mistakes than stay silent waiting for a perfect sentence.

Real conversation first

Every lesson includes live speaking practice — not just listening and filling in blanks.

Progress you can see

Regular check-ins track exactly what's improving, so motivation never has to be a guess.
