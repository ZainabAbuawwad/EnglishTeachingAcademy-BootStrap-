IELTS vs. TOEFL

IELTS
Most recognized in UK & Australia
  • Face-to-face speaking test
  • Paper or computer-based
  • Scored on a 9-band scale
  • Results in 3–5 days

TOEFL iBT
Most recognized in US universities
  • Recorded speaking responses
  • Fully computer-based
  • Scored out of 120 points
  • Results in 4–8 days
