Our services

We help learners speak with real confidence, one lesson at a time

FluentUp is an English academy built around speaking first. We offer a range of services to help learners of all levels improve their conversation skills, from small group classes to private lessons and exam preparation.

What we offer

Four ways to learn, one goal

Group classes

Small groups of up to 6 students, matched by level, with live speaking practice every session.

Private lessons

One-on-one sessions built entirely around your goals, pace, and schedule.

Exam preparation

Focused IELTS and TOEFL prep with practice tests, timing strategy, and scored feedback.

Corporate training

On-site or online English programs for teams — emails, meetings, and client calls.
